Quick Description (Teacher Parent Class Follow Up): This project is a redesign of the website, the idea is to propose a simple app to allow teacher to manage class & student, and the parent to follow their childs

  • Research & Insights: In our research we have take in consideration the existing (the web platform) and the usability through the mobile. We wanted to allow each part to feel confortable with the app. We tool insights from teachers, parents and other member of the association.
  • Solution Design: We implemented screen that display the necessary information, to take the current feature of the web platform and to adapt it to a native app, we don't want to propose only a webview but a true app.
  • Impact: The app is now deployed in test, we are currently testing it in our side but also with our user to have the more accurate insight. The application will be deployed in September 2025.

Quick Description (Hassanates.fr): The goal of those apps is to manage people deeds. For the v1, we were concentrated on the Aid El Adha, which is one of the most important event in Muslim year.

  • Research & Insights: We identified 3 workflows, how people will be able to follow their order, how attributor/butcher will be able to manage and deliver the service, and how manager will be able to follow the evolution.
  • Solution Design: To answer the needs, we have designed 3 distincts apps : one web app for the end user to follow his order, another web app for the manage to help him on his management tasks, and one android app to help attributor/butcher in theirs tasks.
  • Impact: This application is currently in development, the release is planned for June 2025 or earlier, we will try to publish the impact after the Aid el Adha.
