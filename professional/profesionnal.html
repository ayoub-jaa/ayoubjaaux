GEODIS - Office365 Account Provisioning

This project involved designing a web application for the management of Office365 accounts at GEODIS. The main goals were to automate the account provisioning process, reduce errors, and ensure a seamless integration of data between HR and IT systems.

  • Research & Insights: Conducted interviews and workshops with key stakeholders (HR, IT, Managers) to identify pain points in the current process.
  • Solution Design: Created wireframes, prototypes, and user flows, using tools like Adobe XD and Material Design.
  • Impact: The application reduced data entry errors by 97%, streamlined processes, and improved data quality within the HR and IT systems.

The project was developed in phases, first rolling out in France, then expanding globally. I collaborated with the development team to ensure the final product met all user needs and business requirements.

Boursorama Bank - Digital Banking Solutions

At Boursorama Bank, I contributed to the redesign of their customer-facing digital banking solutions. The goal was to enhance the user experience, improve navigation, and provide a more streamlined and intuitive interface for their banking services.

  • User Research: Conducted user testing and gathered insights from both customers and internal stakeholders to define the product requirements.
  • Wireframing & Prototyping: Created high-fidelity wireframes and prototypes in Figma and collaborated with the development team to ensure smooth implementation.
  • Results: The redesign led to a 20% increase in user engagement and a 15% improvement in customer satisfaction scores.

The project also involved extensive testing and iteration based on real user feedback to continually enhance the overall banking experience.
