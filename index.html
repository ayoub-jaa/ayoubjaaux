About me

With over 7 years of experience as a UX Designer and IT professional, I specialize in creating user-centric solutions that bridge the gap between design and technology. My work spans various industries, including logistics, banking, and IT support. Here’s a snapshot of my journey:

  • UX Design Expertise: Designed intuitive interfaces and workflows, including a web app for managing Office365 accounts at GEODIS, which automated processes, reduced errors to nearly zero, and streamlined HR and IT data integration.
  • Leadership: Led a global IT team of 3, introduced effective processes, and ensured delivery of user-focused solutions across diverse business needs.
  • Data Optimization: Co-designed a system to monitor and process HR application data flows, transforming incomprehensible logs into actionable insights through tools like EDI and Power Automate.
  • Problem-Solving: Tackled complex technical challenges, aligning IT systems (e.g., IAM and HR tools) with business objectives, leading to significant improvements in data quality and productivity.

My approach revolves around listening to users, understanding their challenges, and transforming those insights into practical designs.

Beyond my professional work, I dedicate part of my time to volunteering as a UX consultant for non-profits, helping organizations overcome resource limitations while delivering impactful, user-centered solutions.
